Speedcubing!

I'm an competitive speedcuber. I participate in WCA (World Cube Association) competitions about 4-6 times a year, and organize some as well. Though I currently live in California, I'm registered on CubingUSA in my home state of Iowa, where formerly held the state record for 4x4 and 5x5 average .

Was at one point ranked in top 100 nationally for 5x5 single and average. But then people got fast. Current main goal is to get that back.

CubingUSA email parser

The CubingUSA email thingy outputs emails in a way that is not very nice to paste into email boxes to generate an email list. This app parses that raw output and gives you all the emails line by line, either outputted in a text box or directly outputted to a file. The file outputted will be in the same directory as where the app is. Here's the app: Email Parser
